ANALYSIS OF CURRENT STATE, DEVELOPMENT PROSPECTS AND OPTIMIZATION OF PASSENGER TRAFFIC IN THE CITY OF IZHEVSK

KETOVA K. 1,

КОНФЕРЕНЦИЯ:

КЛЮЧЕВЫЕ СЛОВА:
 
BIG DATA, DATA ANALYSIS, OPTIMIZATION, PUBLIC TRANSPORT, ROUTE
АННОТАЦИЯ:
 

A technique is proposed that allows you to analyze and construct a scheme for optimizing public transport routes. The calculations are carried out using the example of the city of Izhevsk in the Udmurt Republic. Intellectual processing of statistical data of the road transport system and characteristics of the functioning of public transport is carried out by Big Data and Data Analysis. A model for optimizing the work of public transport has been developed. The implementation of the model made it possible to construct optimal routes for the movement of buses, trams, trolleybuses, as well as improve the layout of public transport stops.
